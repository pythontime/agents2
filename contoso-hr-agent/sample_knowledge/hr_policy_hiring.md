# Contoso HR Policy: Hiring & Recruitment

## 1. Job Posting Requirements

All open positions must be posted internally for a minimum of 5 business days before external posting. Job descriptions must include: role title, level, required qualifications, preferred qualifications, compensation band (range), location policy (remote/hybrid/on-site), and reporting structure.

Compensation ranges must reflect the approved band for the level. Posting a role without an approved headcount requisition (HC-REQ) is prohibited. HC-REQ approval requires sign-off from the hiring manager, department VP, and HR Business Partner.

## 2. Candidate Screening Process

Initial resume screening must be completed within 5 business days of application receipt. Screeners must evaluate candidates against the job description requirements only. Personal characteristics unrelated to job performance (age, appearance, non-relevant background details) must not factor into screening decisions.

All candidates who apply for the same role must be evaluated against the same criteria. Any deviation requires written justification documented in the ATS (Applicant Tracking System).

## 3. Interview Process

Standard interview process:
1. HR screen (30 min, by HR coordinator or recruiter)
2. Hiring manager interview (45-60 min)
3. Technical or skills assessment (defined per role family)
4. Panel interview with 2-3 team members (45-60 min each)
5. Reference checks (minimum 2 professional references)
6. Offer approval and extension

All interviewers must complete "Interview Foundations" training before conducting interviews. Interview notes must be documented in the ATS within 24 hours of each interview.

## 4. Offer and Onboarding

Verbal offers may not be extended without written approval from HR. Written offer letters must be generated through the official HR system. Offer letters must specify: start date, title, level, compensation, benefits eligibility date, and at-will employment statement.

Background checks are required for all new hires and must be completed before the start date. The scope of the background check depends on role sensitivity (standard, enhanced for financial/security roles).

## 5. Minimum Qualifications Policy

Minimum qualifications listed in a job description are non-negotiable for advancing a candidate. Waivers require VP-level approval and must be documented. "Preferred qualifications" may be waived at hiring manager discretion.

For technical roles, a skills assessment score below 70% automatically disqualifies a candidate from advancing unless an exception is approved and documented.

## 6. Re-Hire Policy

Former employees (in good standing) who separated within the past 3 years may be eligible for expedited hiring. Former employees who were terminated for cause are not eligible for re-hire without CHRO approval.

## 7. Referrals

Employee referrals are encouraged. Referring employees must disclose any personal relationship with the candidate. Referrals are not guaranteed an interview — they must meet minimum qualifications. Referral bonuses are paid 90 days after the referred hire's start date.

## 8. Record Keeping

All hiring documentation (applications, interview notes, assessment results, offer letters) must be retained for 4 years per legal requirements. Electronic records in the ATS satisfy this requirement.
