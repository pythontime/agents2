# Contoso HR Policy: Compensation & Benefits

## 1. Compensation Philosophy

Contoso targets compensation at the 65th percentile of the relevant market for each role family and level. Market data is reviewed annually using third-party salary surveys (Radford, Mercer). Compensation includes base salary, annual performance bonus, equity (for Level 5+), and benefits.

## 2. Salary Bands

Salary bands are set per role family and level. Bands are updated annually in January.

| Level | Band Description       | Annual Base Range (USD) |
|-------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| L1    | Associate              | $52,000 – $72,000       |
| L2    | Professional           | $70,000 – $95,000       |
| L3    | Senior                 | $90,000 – $130,000      |
| L4    | Lead / Staff           | $125,000 – $175,000     |
| L5    | Principal / Manager    | $165,000 – $230,000     |
| L6    | Director               | $210,000 – $290,000     |
| L7    | VP                     | $275,000 – $400,000     |

All offers must be within the approved band. Mid-point targeting is preferred for candidates who fully meet but do not exceed requirements. Above mid-point targeting requires written justification.

## 3. Performance Bonus

Annual performance bonuses are paid in March for the prior calendar year. Target bonus percentages by level:

- L1-L2: 5% of base salary
- L3-L4: 10% of base salary
- L5-L6: 15-20% of base salary
- L7+: 20-30% of base salary

Actual bonus payout is multiplied by individual performance rating (0.0x–1.5x) and company performance multiplier (announced annually).

## 4. Equity

Equity (RSUs) is granted at hire for Level 5 and above. Vesting schedule: 25% cliff at 1 year, then monthly vesting over 3 years. Equity grants require compensation committee approval for L6 and above.

Equity refresh grants may be awarded annually based on performance and retention risk. Refresh grants are not guaranteed.

## 5. Sign-On Bonuses

Sign-on bonuses may be offered to offset forfeited compensation from a prior employer. Sign-on bonuses above $25,000 require VP approval. Sign-on bonuses are subject to clawback if the employee leaves within 12 months of hire.

## 6. Relocation

Contoso offers relocation assistance for roles requiring geographic moves of 50+ miles. Standard relocation package:
- Tier 1 (domestic, rental → rental): Up to $5,000 lump sum
- Tier 2 (domestic, homeowner): Up to $15,000
- Tier 3 (international): Managed relocation, case-by-case

Relocation assistance is subject to clawback if the employee leaves within 18 months.

## 7. Benefits Overview

All regular full-time employees are eligible for benefits beginning on their start date:
- Medical, dental, vision (employee + dependents)
- 401(k) with 4% company match (vested immediately)
- 20 days PTO annually (L1-L4), 25 days (L5+), unlimited PTO for VP+
- 12 weeks paid parental leave (primary caregiver), 4 weeks (secondary caregiver)
- $5,000 annual learning & development budget
- Remote work stipend: $100/month for internet + $500 one-time home office setup

## 8. Pay Transparency

Contoso complies with all applicable pay transparency requirements. Job postings in qualifying jurisdictions include the full salary band. Employees may discuss their compensation with coworkers without retaliation (NLRA-protected).

## 9. Contractor-to-Full-Time Conversions

Contractors converting to full-time must be compensated within the approved band for their level and role family. Prior contract tenure may count toward level placement but does not guarantee above-midpoint placement without documented justification.
